Account & Data Deletion

App: HydraTrack – Water Reminder
Developer: Rylpix

Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly inside the app.

Steps
1. Open the app
2. Go to Settings
3. Tap Delete Account

That’s it.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

When you delete your account:

Your account is removed

Your data is deleted from our system

Deleted data cannot be recovered.

Account deletion happens immediately or within a short period of time.

Data Use

We do not sell user data

We do not share user data
